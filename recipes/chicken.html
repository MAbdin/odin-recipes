Chicken Tikka

Description

A great recipe for healthy protein


Ingredients

    Chopped Chicken Breast
    Desi Dahi Whole Milk Yogurt
    Garlic Paste
    Ginger Paste
    Lemon Juice
    Ground Coriander
    Turmeric
    Cumin
    Paprika
    Red chili powder
    Salt
    Black Pepper

Steps

  • Place the chopped chicken breast in a large bowl.
  • Add yogurt, garlic paste, ginger paste, lemon juice, ground coriander, turmeric, cumin, paprika, red chili powder, salt and black pepper into the bowl.
  • Mix everything together and then cover bowl and let it marinate in the fridge overnight.
  • Thread the chicken onto skewers.
  • Cook on charcoal grill for 10 minutes.
  • Serve with mint chutney.
